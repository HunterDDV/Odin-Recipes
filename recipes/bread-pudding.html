-Steamed Caramel Bread Pudding-

Salted Caramel Bread Pudding is a decadent dessert made with brioche or challah bread, half-n-half, eggs, butter, and brown sugar topped with a silky smooth salted caramel sauce.

Ingredients Needed:

  • ¼ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup cubed bread
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • ½ teaspoon ground coriander

Steps For Cooking:

  1. Melt butter in a small pan over medium heat.
  2. Add 1 cup sugar and stir occasionally until dissolved. Stop stirring once the mixture comes to a boil.
  3. Stay near the stove to monitor the color, swirling the pan gently to redistribute caramel as sugar begins to darken.
  4. Cook to about 310 degrees F (160 degrees C) or until the sugar turns light golden brown, 5 to 10 minutes.
  5. Combine bread cubes, milk, condensed milk, eggs, vanilla, 1 teaspoon sugar, cinnamon, cardamom, and coriander in a blender; puree everything together.
  6. Pour batter on top of caramel.
  7. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
  8. Bring water to a boil.
  9. Cover bread pudding and place in the steamer; cook until a knife comes out clean, 40 to 45 minutes.
  10. Remove from the oven and cool to room temperature, 45 minutes to an hour.
  11. Cool in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
  12. Remove from the refrigerator and turn out onto a plate to serve.
